
# Handoff Frameworks

## Fixed vs Flexible Framework

Use this to prevent over-prescription.

### Fixed
Elements required for strategic integrity:
- audience
- page mission
- conversion goal
- required messages
- critical sections
- required trust proof
- legal or technical constraints

### Flexible
Elements where design craft should lead:
- visual style
- rhythm
- layout refinement
- component expression
- illustration style
- motion
- interaction treatment

---

## Designer Brief Compression Framework

A designer brief should reduce upstream artifacts into:

1. Why this page exists
2. Who it serves
3. What it must accomplish
4. What it must communicate
5. What must be preserved
6. What can be explored
7. What risks remain

---

## Design Review Readiness Framework

Before handoff, confirm:

- stable strategy
- stable page structure
- stable CTA logic
- explicit asset needs
- visible constraints
- clear decision space
- defined review criteria

---

## Asset Dependency Framework

Classify each asset as:

### Required
Cannot execute page properly without it.

### Recommended
Improves quality but not blocking.

### Optional
Enhancement only.

### Missing
Needs owner and timeline.

---

## Brief Quality Framework

A strong brief is:

- specific enough to guide
- flexible enough to design
- rationale-rich
- constraint-aware
- review-ready
