
# Designer Handoff Checklist

## Upstream Inputs

- Page Strategy Document included
- Content-First Page Draft included
- Wireframe Specification included
- Multi-Role Review Report included

---

## Strategic Clarity

- page mission included
- business goal included
- target user included
- narrative strategy included
- CTA strategy included

---

## Designer Usability

- fixed requirements clear
- designer decision space clear
- section rationale included
- visual direction guidance included
- anti-patterns included

---

## Asset Readiness

- required assets listed
- missing assets identified
- fallback options included
- asset dependencies visible

---

## Risk Visibility

- risks listed
- open questions listed
- owners or next actions included
- blockers marked clearly

---

## Review Readiness

- first review criteria included
- expected deliverables included
- breakpoints included
- readiness status declared

---

## Output Validation

- no final UI generated
- no over-prescription
- no hidden assumptions
- no missing critical sections
- no unsupported visual mandates
