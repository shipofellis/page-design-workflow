
# Example Designer Handoff Excerpt

## Page Mission

Help operations managers understand that AI agents can automate repetitive workflows safely and quickly.

---

## Fixed Requirements

- Hero must communicate automation outcome, not feature list.
- Primary CTA must remain "Book a demo".
- Trust content must appear before the second CTA.
- Setup simplicity must be addressed before final CTA.

---

## Designer Decision Space

The designer may explore:
- split hero vs centered hero
- product screenshot vs workflow diagram
- calm enterprise SaaS style vs sharper technical style
- motion for workflow explanation

---

## Rationale

Users are interested in automation outcomes but skeptical about reliability.
The design must therefore balance ambition with operational trust.
