
# Workflow Retrospective Checklist

## Input Coverage

- designer handoff brief reviewed
- upstream artifacts reviewed when available
- feedback sources reviewed
- metrics reviewed when available

---

## Evidence Integrity

- evidence separated from opinion
- confidence labeled
- unsupported claims avoided
- missing evidence identified

---

## Learning Quality

- what worked identified
- what failed identified
- reusable patterns extracted
- anti-patterns captured
- recurring gaps identified

---

## Assumption Management

- assumptions listed
- validation status assigned
- follow-up actions defined
- unknowns preserved

---

## SOP Improvement Quality

- recommendations step-specific
- recommendations evidence-linked
- recommendations actionable
- priority assigned

---

## Output Validation

- not just a summary
- no invented metrics
- no vague process advice
- reusable assets include reuse conditions
- next-step readiness declared
