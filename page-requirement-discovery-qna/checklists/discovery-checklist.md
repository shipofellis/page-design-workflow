
# Discovery Checklist

## Question Quality

Every question:
- has purpose
- maps to a gap
- affects downstream decisions
- avoids vagueness
- minimizes cognitive load

---

## Interview Efficiency

- question count controlled
- no duplicate questions
- blockers prioritized first
- low-value questions removed

---

## State Integrity

- resolved gaps updated
- unresolved gaps preserved
- assumptions explicit
- contradictions tracked
- readiness updated

---

## Output Validation

- no hallucinated answers
- no hidden assumptions
- no skipped blockers
- structured outputs only
