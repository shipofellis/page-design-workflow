
# Section Design Frameworks

## Hero Section

Purpose:
- establish value quickly
- orient user
- define primary action

Should answer:
- what is this?
- why should I care?
- what should I do next?

---

## Trust Section

Purpose:
- reduce skepticism
- establish legitimacy
- support CTA readiness

Possible assets:
- logos
- testimonials
- metrics
- certifications

---

## Problem Section

Purpose:
- increase problem awareness
- create emotional relevance
- frame urgency

---

## Solution Section

Purpose:
- explain mechanism
- connect value to workflow
- reduce ambiguity

---

## Objection Handling Section

Purpose:
- reduce hesitation
- address adoption concerns
- unblock conversion

---

## CTA Reinforcement Section

Purpose:
- consolidate confidence
- simplify action
- reduce friction
