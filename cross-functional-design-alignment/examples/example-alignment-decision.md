
# Example Alignment Decision

## Conflict

PM wants three CTA placements above the fold to maximize conversion.
Designer argues this creates visual noise and weakens comprehension.
Frontend notes sticky CTA on mobile may conflict with cookie banner and bottom navigation.

## Tradeoff

Conversion pressure vs clarity and mobile usability.

## Decision

Use one primary CTA in the hero and one secondary text link.
Add the next primary CTA after the trust/proof section.
Do not use sticky CTA in v1.

## Rationale

The user is still evaluating trust in the first screen. Too much CTA pressure may reduce credibility.

## Accepted Risk

Lower immediate CTA density may reduce early clicks.

## Mitigation

Track hero CTA click-through and scroll depth. Reconsider sticky CTA after launch data.

## Owner

PM owns measurement plan.
Designer owns CTA visual hierarchy.
Frontend owns mobile feasibility check.
