
# Alignment Workshop Guide

## Recommended Agenda

### 1. Confirm Objective
Clarify the goal: move from critique to agreed execution plan.

### 2. Review Artifacts
Review page strategy, content-first draft, wireframe specification, and multi-role review report.

### 3. Consolidate Concerns
Group concerns by strategy, UX, conversion, brand, frontend, architecture, accessibility, and scope.

### 4. Identify Conflicts
Explicitly name conflicts and affected roles.

### 5. Decide Tradeoffs
For each major conflict:
- list options
- evaluate impact
- choose decision
- record accepted risk

### 6. Define Boundaries
Agree on what is fixed, what can change, what is out of scope, and what needs owner follow-up.

### 7. Confirm Handoff Readiness
Decide ready, partially ready, or blocked.

## Facilitation Rules

- Do not allow vague agreement
- Do not skip unresolved conflict
- Do not let preferences override evidence without acknowledgment
- Assign owners to every open item
