
# Common Narrative Frameworks

## Framework 1 — Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA

Best for:
- pain-aware users
- workflow products
- productivity tools

---

## Framework 2 — Outcome → Mechanism → Trust → CTA

Best for:
- AI products
- technical products
- innovative products

---

## Framework 3 — Awareness → Education → Conviction → Conversion

Best for:
- new categories
- unfamiliar technology
- emerging markets

---

## Framework 4 — Pain → Differentiation → Validation → Action

Best for:
- competitive markets
- switching-focused products
- comparison pages

---

## Framework Selection Rules

Choose based on:
- user awareness
- market maturity
- complexity
- conversion friction
- trust requirements
