
# Example

## Product

AI workflow assistant for operations teams.

---

## Page Mission

Help operations managers understand that repetitive workflows can be automated safely with AI.

---

## Narrative Model

Outcome → Mechanism → Trust → CTA

Reason:
Users are interested in outcomes first, but require trust before adopting automation tools.

---

## Section Architecture

1. Hero
2. Workflow outcomes
3. How the AI works
4. Example automations
5. Trust and safety
6. Customer proof
7. CTA

---

## Strategic Insight

Trust sections appear earlier than in typical SaaS pages because workflow automation introduces perceived operational risk.
